How to play

Make every colour into one edge-connected group.

Use a rotation icon at an internal corner to turn its surrounding four squares clockwise.

The number below the board is your move count. Each puzzle is solvable in seven or fewer moves.

The 1–4 button shows or hides a number for each colour.

"Cheat" restores the puzzle to its initial state, and illustrates one possible solution.
